~Coffin-Lowry Syndrome | National Institute of Neurological Disorders and stroke. Coffin-Lowry Syndrome. (n.d.). https://www.ninds.nih.gov/health-information/disorders/coffin-lowry-syndrome

-- Summary: Coffin-Lowry syndrome is a genetic disorder characterized by physical and mental deformities. Some facial features that are the result of Coffin-Lowry are slanted eyes, broad nose, and large ears. Neurological complications include seizures, hearing, speech, movement coordination, and the loss of the ability to walk. Coffin-Lowry syndrome is a rare disorder so doctors and researchers do not have much information about it.
